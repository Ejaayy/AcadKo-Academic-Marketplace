About Us

Welcome to AcadKo! We're a platform created by students, for students, to revolutionize the way academic resources are exchanged.

Our Mission

At AcadKo, our mission is to empower students by providing a marketplace where they can easily buy and sell academic resources. Whether you're looking for textbooks, notes, or tutoring services, we've got you covered.

Our Vision

We aim to create a community where students can share knowledge and resources, enabling better learning experiences for everyone. Our platform helps reduce the barriers to academic success, making education more accessible and affordable.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of passionate students who believe in the power of collaboration. We come from diverse backgrounds, but we all share a common goal: to improve the academic journey for students everywhere.
